A Little Bit About Me

Not only am I a certified Jedi Web Developer, but I am also an artist and an educator. Originally from Boise Idaho, I received my MFA and BFA from Boise State University. I was a Visiting Assistant Professor of Art at Whitman College in Walla Walla, Washington from Spring 2008-Fall 2013. My work has been in numerous solo and group exhibitions throughout the Northwest, and has been featured in New York, Kentucky, North Carolina, Missouri, and the United Arab Emirates.
